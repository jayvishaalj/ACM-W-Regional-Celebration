About ACM-W

What is ACM-W ?

The Association for Computing Machinery's Council on Women in Computing (ACM-W) supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women. ACM-W is an active organization with over 36,000 members.

Dr Mukta Paliwal

AWS certified ML Specialist, Persistent Systems

Dr Mukta Paliwal is an AWS certified ML Specialist associated with Persistent Systems for five years where she has handled various roles including Project Lead, Senior Team Lead, Consultant Analytics, etc. She was a lecturer in Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, Maharashtra. Panelists

Shachi Dave

Research Engineer Google Research India

Shachi Dave is a Software Engineer in the Natural Language Understanding group at Google Research India. At the University of Southern California, she received her Master's degree in Computer Science. Shachi worked at several startups in the U.S. before joining Google. She is active in the fields of natural language understanding, conversational AI and data mining/modelling. Her research article "Interlingua-based English–Hindi machine translation and language divergence" has gained a lot of attention.

Mr Anudeep Jain

Senior Manager-Product development, FIS Global

Mr Anudeep Jain is managing development for treasury solution and is the product owner for business in integration with FIS flagship product called Front Arena. He is also managing teams of functional and technical experts to deliver high-quality, practical solutions. Mr Jain has spent the last 13+ years building financial solutions. He has an excellent knowledge of treasury solution, position keeping systems, back-office system, various asset classes and their valuations, CDS pricing, swift messaging, various regulatory reporting and has been contributing in said fields tremendously.

Ms Neelima Vobugari

CEO, Tarah AI

Neelima Vobugari is currently CEO of Tarah Technologies, a marquee AI and Machine Learning competency and consulting firm. She is a certified CRM consultant and a certified Data Scientist. She is an alumnus of John Hopkins University, Maryland, where she finished her specialization in Data Sciences. Her IT experience span giant IT companies including IBM. She has recently been conferred the prestigious "Women in AI" award given to select women leaders in data science and AI. She is a strong innovator, has filed three patents in the area of AI, and providing innovative solutions to over 20 AI consulting projects at marquee clients.

Ms Kalika Bali

Principal Researcher, Microsoft Research Labs

Ms Kalika Bali is a researcher at Microsoft Research India working in the areas of Machine Learning, Natural Language Systems and Applications, as well as Technology for Emerging Markets. She is currently working on Project Mélange where they try to understand, process and generate Code-mixed language data for both text and speech. In her own words “Linguistics is one subject that goes from absolute science to absolute philosophy.” Her research interests lie broadly in the area of Speech and Language Technology, especially in the use of linguistic models for building technology that offers a more natural Human-Computer as well as Computer-Mediated interactions.

Dr. Chitra Babu, Professor and Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering has 20 years of teaching and research experience.
